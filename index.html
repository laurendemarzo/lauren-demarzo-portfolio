About

Health communication rooted in empathy and evidence

I am a dedicated health communication writer and strategist with 15 years of experience creating clear, compassionate cancer education resources at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. I apply plain language principles and health literacy best practices to translate complex clinical evidence into content that supports patients and caregivers at every stage of their health journey.

I thrive in collaborative environments — building partnerships with subject matter experts, clinical teams, design and product teams, and agency partners to bring high-quality health content to life across print and digital channels.

My work is grounded in a belief that greater health knowledge empowers every person — regardless of age, background, or reading level — to make confident decisions about their care.

Published Digital Content

MSK Disease Guides — Contributing Writer

As a Senior Health Education Specialist at MSK, I wrote, fact-checked, and migrated content for the MSK Disease Guides. The MSK Disease Guides are collections of person-centered digital resources about cancer, how it is found, treated, and continued care programs people need. Drawing on my background in integrative medicine and survivorship services, I was regularly assigned to lead the Continued Care sections across multiple disease groups. I collaborated with clinical subject matter experts, design teams, and product teams across all phases of production.
